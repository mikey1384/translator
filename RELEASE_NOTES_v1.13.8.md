# v1.13.8

## Title
sharper AI recommendations, stronger regional search, and cleaner subtitle handling

## Release Notes

- Reworked AI video recommendations so search results stay visible while seeds stream in, follow-up searches feel more persistent, and the recommendation UI is simpler with clearer progress and less clutter.
- Strengthened country and locale handling for video search: regional bias now better preserves market targeting and local-language query planning, including planner fallbacks and direct searches.
- Preserved saved recommendation setup across app updates by migrating older local preference keys forward instead of silently resetting country, recency, and topic settings.
- Improved subtitle and player workflow: mounted subtitle files keep their video association more reliably, and the side menu can now unmount or delete mounted subtitle entries directly.
