# v1.13.5

## Title
more reliable dubbing, smarter video recommendations, and stronger download recovery

## Release Notes

- Improved dubbing reliability so long voice dubs are less likely to stop early or fail on transient relay interruptions.
- Fixed several local-video transcription blockers, including cases where duration detection or video analysis could get stuck before transcription started.
- Made AI video recommendation feel more persistent and useful: search state survives page switches, follow-up suggestions are smarter, and download history/channels are easier to revisit.
- Improved download recovery and history behavior so saved copies are preserved, temp copies are labeled more clearly, and metadata like titles, thumbnails, and channels is recorded more reliably.
- Upgraded error reports with better context from the app and main process so user reports are much more actionable when something goes wrong.
- Cleaned up parts of the player and workflow UI, including better side-menu actions, progress handling, and tighter action layouts in history cards.
